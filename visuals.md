# FARDI — Visuals Generation Reference

**Platform theme:** FARDI is a gamified CEFR English language learning platform for Tunisian university students. The visual style is clean, modern, flat illustration — think Duolingo meets a professional academic app. Primary colors: blue `#1976d2`, gold `#f5a623`, green `#4caf50`. Backgrounds are white or light grey. All visuals should feel encouraging, professional, and slightly playful.

Copy each **Gemini Prompt** directly into Gemini to generate the asset.
